## Linux FS

* ext
* ext2: no journal: for small spaces
* ext3: journal
* ext4: last version of ext FS
* reiserfs
* jfs
* xfs

## FS from others OS

* FAT: legacy from dos/windows 95. file size limited to 4GO. works well with linux, mac, windws
* NTFS: from windows 2000
* HFS: hierarchical FS
